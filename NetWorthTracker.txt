Finalized Features & Logic
1. Assets Management

Liquid Assets: Cash, Bank Accounts (these change with every transaction)
Investments: Stocks, Mutual Funds, FDs, Crypto
Physical Assets: Property, Vehicle, Jewelry, Electronics
Auto-update: When you add income/expense, your primary bank/cash asset automatically adjusts

2. Liabilities Management

Credit Cards (balance, limit, due date, minimum payment)
Loans (EMI amount, remaining principal, interest rate, tenure left)
Other debts
Auto-update: When you pay EMI/bill, both cash and liability decrease

3. Transaction System

Income: Adds to your selected asset (usually bank account)
Expense: Deducts from your selected asset
Transfer: Move money between assets (doesn't affect net worth)
Each transaction specifies: amount, category, date, from/to which account

4. Recurring Transactions (Auto-tracking)

Income: Salary (monthly), freelance payments, rental income
Expenses: Rent, EMIs, subscriptions, bills, insurance
Auto-create: System prompts/auto-adds these on schedule
Due reminders: Shows upcoming bills/EMIs on dashboard

5. Budget Management

Set monthly limits per category (Food: ₹10k, Transport: ₹5k, etc.)
Track spending vs budget in real-time
Alerts when approaching/exceeding limits
Rollover unused budget option

6. Goal Tracking

Set savings goals (Emergency fund: ₹5L, House: ₹20L, Vacation: ₹2L)
Track progress automatically based on net worth growth
Calculate: "Save ₹X/month to reach goal by deadline"
Option to allocate specific assets to specific goals

7. Bills & EMI Tracker

All recurring expenses with due dates
Payment status (paid/pending)
Auto-mark as paid when transaction added
Upcoming dues calendar view

8. Dashboard Analytics

Net Worth: Real-time calculation
Cash Flow: Monthly income - expenses = savings
Savings Rate: (Savings / Income) × 100
Expense Breakdown: Pie chart by category
Net Worth Trend: Graph over time
Budget Health: Category-wise spending vs budget
Debt-to-Asset Ratio: Total liabilities / Total assets

9. Reports & Insights

Monthly/yearly financial summary
Spending trends (which categories increasing/decreasing)
Income growth tracking
Best/worst spending months
Category comparison across months

10. Smart Features

Primary Account: Designate one bank account where salary comes and expenses go (auto-adjusts with transactions)
Transaction Categories: Pre-defined + custom categories
Search & Filter: Find any transaction by date, amount, category, keyword
Export Data: Download as CSV/PDF
Data Backup: Auto-save to browser storage
